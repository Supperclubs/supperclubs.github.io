What is this?

A regular(ish) group of ~10 people who want to get to know each other and keep this going.

Once a month, at home. Cosy, quiet. A semi-structured conversation about a big idea, guided by a text we read together.

01

We pick a topic

A big question worth wrestling with over dinner.

— something that matters

02

We share a reading

A text, article, or excerpt to ground the conversation.

— common ground

03

We gather and talk

No lectures, no winners. Just honest exchange.

— the good part

The kind of questions we ask

For example...

"Should everyone have the right to vote, regardless of knowledge or engagement?"
"Should future generations have political representation today?"
"What idea feels hardest for you to defend out loud?"
"What would you lose if society became more just?"
