About

What is Supper Club?

A regular(ish) group of ~10 people who want to get to know each other and keep this going. Once a month, at home. Cosy, quiet. A semi-structured conversation about a big idea, guided by a text we read together.

There are no lectures, no winners. Just honest exchange between people willing to think carefully and listen generously.

How it works

We pick a topic — a big question worth wrestling with over dinner. Something that matters, that resists easy answers.

We share a reading — a text, article, or excerpt to ground the conversation. Everyone reads it beforehand so we start from common ground.

We gather and talk — no lectures, no winners. Just honest exchange. The conversation is guided but not controlled. The goal is understanding, not agreement.

Who is it for?

People who are curious. People who enjoy ideas but also enjoy people. You don't need to be an expert on anything — you just need to be willing to engage thoughtfully and listen well.

We keep the group small so everyone has space to speak and be heard.

Interested?

If this speaks to you, get in touch. We're always happy to hear from kindred spirits.
